# SmartBill PWA - Version 2 Roadmap

## Overview

Version 2 focuses on enhancing productivity, automation, reporting, and business intelligence while keeping the application simple and lightweight. These features are intentionally excluded from Version 1 to ensure a fast MVP release and quicker customer validation.

---

# Productivity Features

## Quick Add Products

* Pin the most frequently sold products on the home screen.
* Add products with a single tap.
* Customizable product shortcuts.

---

## Favorite Products

* Mark products as favorites.
* Display favorites at the top of product search.
* Allow shop owners to reorder favorite products.

---

## Smart Product Suggestions

* Suggest related products based on previous sales.
* Example:

  * LED Bulb → Bulb Holder
  * Wire → Electrical Tape
  * Switch → Switch Board

---

## Recently Sold Products

* Display recently billed products for quick selection.
* One-tap addition to the invoice.

---

## Frequently Used Customers

* Maintain a list of recent customers.
* Auto-fill customer details during billing.

---
